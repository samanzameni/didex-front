Step 3

Identity Proof

Please provide a picture of any of the following:

  • Passport (the main double-page spread)
  • Driver's license (both sides)
  • National identity document (both sides).

Photo/scan must be clear, in color and display the entire ID.

Your full legal name and date of birth entered in form "Personal information" must exactly match the information in your document.

Documents in non-latin characters cannot be accepted without certified translation.
